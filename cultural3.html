Analysis of Artifact

The iPhone also keeps fashion by its looking. The original look of it is simple and clear. Simple is beautiful. At the same time, many designers make iPhone with variable cases. Cases are not only a protection, but also a fashion and representation personality. Type, camera, or animal shape cases are beautiful and characteristic to see. For example, some girls using shining crystals stick on their phone to make iPhone look cute. Business people use leather cases to present serious. I uses a case looks like a book. The reason is it looks cool and it has card cases inside. Therefore, I do not need to carry lots of things in my hand. Also, the case is very protectable for my phone. I am always careless and drop my iPhone on the floor; it gives a strong protection for it. I think my case is a good example to represent me, a person wants easy life and careless.

Another reason iPhone be popular is because of the technology. IPhone almost carry the newest technology with each new version present. Clear display, fast CPU, multi-touch screen and so on make iPhone become the highest technology represent of phone. For example, the SIRI, it is like a private robot assistant. People can just ask SIRI for help as asking a person. The multi-touch function makes control a phone like a computer. It is easy to do. Instead of using only the thumb to type, using all fingers is easier to control. And with different numbers of finger, it works differently. Fingerprints can keep information secured while the phone is lost or stolen.

In addition, iPhone makes thing convenience. It contained lots of function in the iPhone. Instead of carrying many things to get out, it is easy to just bring a phone. And it can use as a camera, a music player, a recorder, an e-book and a map. One of the convenience function iPhone contains is APP. It let other station could work on a small phone, like camera, game player, record, editor, computer and dictionary. For example, a dictionary app I used often is called YOUDAO. It is easy to use to translate between English, Chinese and Japanese. It also contains a useful and fun translation function called camera dictionary. It can catch the word by camera.
